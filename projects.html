Protocol – Build Habits with Intention

Protocol is a minimalist iOS app designed to help you build habits, track your mood, and reflect daily. It combines a habit tracker, mood log, and reflection journal into one simple interface, empowering you to move forward with clarity and consistency.

Built entirely with SwiftUI, Protocol offers a clean, distraction-free experience without ads or clutter. It's your personal system for self-improvement, designed to support your growth—one day at a time.
