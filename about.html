About Me

My Journey into iOS Development

Born in Uzbekistan, raised with purpose. I wasn’t chasing code — I was chasing clarity. After years of questioning systems, and walking the path, I found something in iOS development that felt right. Swift and SwiftUI weren’t just tools — they were frameworks to build real, lasting things with speed, elegance, and control.

I taught myself, launched my first app Protocol, and put everything into learning with depth. I value clean architecture, deep focus, and purposeful design. I don’t chase titles or trends — I chase the craft. Every line of code I write respects the user and the future I’m building toward.

Gratitude

I stand on the shoulders of people who believed in me before the world did. Some invested their time. Some invested their money. Some invested their faith.

But one of them did all three.

In 2022, I was a teenager with fire but no structure — vision but no direction.
He saw something in me that even I hadn’t fully seen yet.
He sponsored my path to becoming an iOS developer. He gave me belief — and accountability.

His name is not here yet. Because I want to earn the right to name him.
But I carry his impact in every commit, every product, every user I serve.

This site, these apps, this journey — all of it traces back to that seed.
And this is just the beginning.

His guidance wasn’t just technical. It was foundational. And I carry that with me, quietly but proudly.
